PHAN 7 -- TRAIL KEEPER (MOI v17)
================================================================================

Trail Keeper la nguoi dan dia phuong tao va ban
"Trail Experience Product" -- khong chi file GPX ma
gom 5 lop:
  Lop 1: Route       (GPX -- tinh, copy duoc)
  Lop 2: Conditions  (mua, thoi tiet -- nguoi dia phuong biet)
  Lop 3: Moments     (khoanh khac -- vuon dao thang 2, suong mu 5h30)
  Lop 4: Knowledge   (bi mat ban dia -- nha chi A cho tru mua)
  Lop 5: Freshness   (live layer -- dieu kien tuan nay)

TEP SCORE = Route x Conditions x Moments x Knowledge x Freshness
Score cao hon -> xep hang cao hon -> doanh thu cao hon

PHAN DOANH THU:
  Trail Keeper: 60%  |  HLRace: 40%
  Tip tu runner: 100% ve tay keeper (HLRace khong lay)

CHONG COPY:
  - File GPX nhung watermark an: userId + timestamp mua
  - Gia tri that o live layer -- ban copy khong co
  - Tuong lai: NFT trail ownership tren Viction
